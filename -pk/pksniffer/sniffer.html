XPath/CSS Sniffer Bookmarklet Generator

Drag the following link to your browser's bookmarks bar. Then, navigate to any webpage and click the bookmarklet to activate the sniffer.

Once activated, click on any element on the page to get its XPath, CSS Selector, and Classes. The information will be displayed in an alert and logged to the console.

Drag this to your bookmarks bar: Sniff Element

How to use:

  1. Save this page (`sniffer.html`) locally.
  2. Open this `sniffer.html` file in your browser.
  3. Drag the "Sniff Element" link above to your browser's bookmarks bar.
  4. Navigate to any webpage you want to inspect.
  5. Click the "Sniff Element" bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar.
  6. Click on any element on the page. An alert will pop up with its XPath, CSS Selector, and Classes. The details will also be logged to your browser's console.
  7. Click the bookmarklet again to deactivate.
